ABOUT US.

Linggis is an English tutoring platform dedicated to helping individuals achieve proficient language skills. We believe that English proficiency is the key to unlocking global opportunities, whether in education, career advancement, or everyday life. With a team of experienced instructors and innovative teaching methods, Linggis is committed to providing an effective, enjoyable, and personalized learning experience tailored to each student's needs. Our mission is to empower learners by equipping them with the language tools necessary for success in a globalized world.

We offer a variety of programs designed to meet specific needs, including exam preparation courses, conversation classes, and professional training. Each program is crafted to ensure that students gain practical and applicable skills that can be used in real-world scenarios. With a personalized and flexible approach, Linggis ensures that every student can learn comfortably and achieve their English language goals with confidence. Our dedicated support system and interactive learning environment foster a sense of community and encourage continuous progress, making the journey of mastering English both rewarding and enjoyable.

OUR MISSION

The mission of Linggis is to empower individuals to master the English language through effective and enjoyable approaches. We are committed to providing a learning experience tailored to each student's needs, utilizing innovative teaching methods and the latest technology. By equipping students with essential language skills, we help them unlock global opportunities in various fields, including education, career advancement, and everyday life.

We also strive to create a supportive and interactive learning environment where every student feels motivated and valued. Through specially designed programs, we ensure that every student can learn comfortably and confidently. Linggis continuously innovates to enhance the quality of teaching and provides ongoing support, making the journey of learning English a rewarding and fulfilling experience.

OUR VISSION

At Linggis, our vision is to be a leader in providing innovative and meaningful English language education to individuals worldwide. We aspire to create a global community that can communicate fluently and understand each other through the English language. We believe that with strong language skills, individuals can unlock international opportunities and enhance their overall life experiences.

We are committed to being a dynamic learning center where students can develop their confidence and language abilities to the fullest. Our vision is to inspire and guide each student to reach their highest potential in English, enabling them to become successful lifelong learners who contribute positively to an increasingly interconnected global society. Guided by this vision, we are dedicated to continually innovating and improving our learning experience for the benefit of our students and the broader world.
