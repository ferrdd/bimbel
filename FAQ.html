What is Linggish?

Linggish is an English language tutoring platform designed to help users improve their English language skills through various interactive and enjoyable learning methods.

What features does Linggish offer?

Linggish offers a variety of features, including online classes with experienced instructors, quizzes, lessons, learning materials, and much more. Users can also access tailored learning content to suit their needs.

How do I sign up for Linggish?

You can sign up for Linggish by creating a free account on the login and register page. After creating an account, you will be given the option to try out some features for free or pay to access full access to all our features and content. We offer a free demo for some specific classes and materials so users can try before deciding to subscribe. This allows you to get an overview of the learning experience at Linggish before subscribing.

Is Linggish suitable for all levels of English?

Yes, Linggish is designed for all levels, from beginners to advanced levels. We have programs suitable for every skill level, so everyone can learn and develop according to their needs.

How can I track my learning progress on Linggish?

You can track your learning progress on Linggish through your Profile Menu, Lessons, and Learning Materials dashboard. There, you will find statistics that include progress in lessons.

How do I contact Linggish support team?

You can contact our support team through our social media pages located on the page, and questions can be asked on the right side of the FAQ page. Our support team is ready to assist you with any questions or issues you may have.
